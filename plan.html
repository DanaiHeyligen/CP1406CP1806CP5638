Website Name : Enviro Stewards

o Goal

The goal of this website is providing the services to each industry which is polluting the air, water or land. Almost every industry has its waste material which they throw in open air, which is the cause of pollution. Our mission is to provide them services from which they can ensure their wastes as pollutant free in open environment, so as to minimize and control the pollution.

o Success evaluation

I have provided all the solutions to their problem. Customer (Industry) will visit our website, they select their category from services section that in which section their industry fall. After that they get explanation that how they are polluting the environment and how they can get rid from it. This is the process of this website.

o Target audience

Our targeted audience is energy industry, food industry, steel industry, mechanical industry and water engineering industry. Each type of industry is categorize in our services sub-sections. Each industry fall in sub-sections of our services like lca management, footprinting, biodiversity etc.
