About us

Enviro-Stewards focuses on sustainability; prevention, resource conservation, and improving bottom line. ES is playing its part in developing a world more sustainable, conservable, natural, ecological balanced, and peaceful avoiding depletion of natural resources. Sustainability in textile, leather, and sports goods industries is the only way forward and absolute essential in future.
Enviro Steward as a sustainability platform offers a consultative and analytical approach in order to implement practical, measurable, and economically feasible solutions to guide partner factories to produce sustainable products for long-term financial success and business sustainability.
Our process is collaborative and designed to pair the expertise of factory’s team with our experts to achieve the best outcomes possible.
With our extensive networks and collaborations, we promote our partner factories to International Brands and Retailers. Ultimately promoting Pakistan as future’s best Global Sustainable Products Resource.
Enviro Stewards provides wide range of consultancy Services to Textile, Apparel, Home Fashion, Leather, Shoes Sportswear and Sports Goods Industries in;

  • Baseline Studies
  • Gap Analysis.
  • Trainings & Workshops.
  • CAP Implementation.
  • All Certifications; Quality, Social, Environment, and Technical systems
  • Sustainable Product Design & Development.
  • Theme & Content Development, Branding and PR.
  • Productivity Improvement, Lean Manufacturing
  • Industry 4.0.
  • Quality 4.0.
  • IIoT (Industrial Internet of Things).
  • Smart & Virtual Factories.
  • AL (Artificial Intelligence).

ES aims to achieve tangible improvements by real-time monitoring of Social, Environmental and Technical compliances through Automation, Digitization using technology, up-gradation and capacity building of employees in organizations, to achieve business objectives without unnecessary environmental harm and negative impact on people and communities associated with their business activities. ES wants to be a leading Sustainability Service provider by adding value to its client organizations, brands and retailers through Advisory, Training, Audit and Monitoring based on Ethical groundwork.

Our Mission

To be leading sustainable service providing company by adding values to its client organizations. Our sustainability mission envisions meeting the needs of our customers while conducting business in a socially, economically and environmentally responsible manner to the benefit our current and future generations, thereby creating value for all the stake holders.

Our Vission

Enviro Steward aims to become trust worthy partner in the field of Sustainability Linking the UN SDGs to life cycle impact pathway frameworks.
to achieve tangible improvements and real time KPI monitoring of Social, Environmental, and Technical compliances through automation and use of technology /up-gradation and capacity development of employees in organizations supporting them to achieve their business objective and organizational goals.
